## Open

### Author Alignment Gate

Ask near the start, after the first manuscript/bibliography pass and before
heavy fetching:

1. Are these the right research questions for the literature review?
2. Which fields or literatures should anchor the related-work section?
3. Which types of related papers should be included or de-emphasized
   (lab experiments, field experiments, theory, surveys, policy papers, local
   context, methods papers)?
4. Which papers are must-cite or must-not-miss?
5. Which adjacent literatures should we explicitly exclude for now?

Move answered items to `Resolved`; put nonblocking defaults in
`ASSUMPTIONS.md`.
